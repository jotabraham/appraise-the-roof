How to Play

In the Appraise the Roof game, first you are prompted to submit a city and state (i.e. Grand Rapids, MI). Upon submission, the game populates a random selection of 10 houses. Your task is to "Appraise the Roof!" Guess the listed sale price and submit your answer. You are awarded points based on the accuracy of your appraisal.

If you are stumped and would like a hint, they are provided. However, each hint costs valuable points that reduce your final score. After 10 appraisals, submit your High Score to see how you rank as an Appraisal Expert! The highest possible score is 1,000 points (unless, you get an EXACT appraisal - play again and again to see what happens).

And remember, these homes are actually for sale! Hit the SIMple diamond on a home of your liking to save it to your own Favorites section (where we provide addition details and a link to the listing).
